Faith-Based Recovery Housing for Veterans

Located in Lakewood, Ohio, Domus Sobrietatis Ministry Trust provides safe sober housing, spiritual mentorship, and recovery support for veterans rebuilding their lives.

Our Mission

We believe recovery requires community, accountability, and faith. Our ministry exists to provide veterans with a stable environment where they can rebuild their lives through sobriety, purpose, and service.
