Faith-Based Recovery Housing for Veterans

Domus Sobrietatis Ministry Trust exists to provide safe sober living, spiritual encouragement, structure, and long-term recovery support for veterans rebuilding their lives in Lakewood, Ohio.

About Our Mission

We believe recovery requires stability, accountability, community, and purpose. Our ministry supports veterans through faith-based sober living and recovery-centered programming designed to help them move toward long-term independence and wholeness.

What We Provide

Our goal is to create a structured and supportive environment where residents can heal, rebuild, and move forward with dignity. We focus on safe housing, clear expectations, peer support, and spiritual growth.
