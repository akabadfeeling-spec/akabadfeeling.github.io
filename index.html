About Our Mission

Domus Sobrietatis Ministry Trust exists to provide a stable, faith-based recovery environment where veterans can pursue sobriety, restore structure, and move toward long-term independence.

Why This Exists

Too many veterans leave structured environments with nowhere stable to go. Domus Sobrietatis exists to bridge that gap by providing structure, accountability, spiritual encouragement, and a path forward.

Our goal is to create a safe and supportive home where residents can rebuild with dignity, restore healthy routines, and move toward lasting recovery.

What We Believe: Recovery requires more than a bed. It requires stability, accountability, community, purpose, and a consistent environment where progress can take root.

Program Structure

We believe credibility comes from consistency. Our housing model is built around order, responsibility, and clear standards.

  • Zero tolerance for drugs and alcohol
  • Curfew enforced: 10:00 PM on weekdays and 11:00 PM on weekends
  • Weekly house meetings and consistent accountability
  • Mandatory participation in recovery-focused programming
  • Shared responsibilities, chores, and respect for the home environment
  • Expectation of personal growth, discipline, and community participation
